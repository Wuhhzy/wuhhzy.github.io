About

My first major project, built on ROBLOX using Lua and accumulated over 1.8 million visits. An Action RPG where you roam a large map with different areas that was developed over the course of 2+ years, levelling up to unlock new Digimon to transform into. At times we had over 10 members working on the game concurrently, with me as the co-lead developer and eventually lead developer - responsible for coordinating the team, making core design choices and owning the majority of the gameplay scripting. The game generated over 350,000 Robux in revenue - roughly $1,000 USD through Roblox's Developer Exchange program.
